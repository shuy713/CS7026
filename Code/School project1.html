5-A Side Blind Football Auxiliary Training Equipment

Design description

Because of the denpendency on coaches and other reasons, I found that blind football players are very inefficient in training
In order to solve this problem, I designed a set of training aids and interactive interface to help blind athletes to better carry out daily training
