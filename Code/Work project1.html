98 foot Teochew electric cruise

Design descriptione

Chaozhou has a long history, human spirit, has the reputation of "Linghai famous Bang", known by experts and scholars as "Central Plains classical culture window", the cruise ship is to provide transportation for tourists to enjoy the scenery of the Han River, feel the long history and culture of the ancient city of Chaozhou. "Chaozhou" tourist ship is a new energy cruise ship, made of steel and aluminum, carrying 88 passengers.The appearance combines the characteristics of Chaozhou Ancient City, Hanjiang River and Guangji Bridge to carry forward traditional culture, and the content of activities on the ship integrates local traditional activities and intangible cultural heritage.

Indoor Event Planning

Session 1 :Performance of Gongfu Tea Art (National Intangible Heritage)

Activity 1:

Gongfu tea art performed by professionals

Activity 2:

Taste congou tea; Trendy dim sum (miscellaneous, meat, vegetarian)

Session 2 :Performing Chaozhou Opera (NICH)

Activity 1:

Professional performance of local traditional show: "Su Liuniang"; Traditional
folding drama "Window Sweeping Meeting";

Activity 2:

Send gifts; Ask questions about the content related to Chaozhou opera, and get
small prizes by punching the card to take photos and answer questions; Group photo

Session 3 :Chaozhou iron branch puppet show(NICH)

Activity 1:

Professionals perform iron branch puppets, accompanied by singing of Chaozhou opera, and introduce their evolution and history and culture

Activity 2:

Interactive mini games:Experience how to manipulate iron puppets; Punch in to take pictures; Buy related cultural and creative products

Session 4:Play and sing tidal songs(NICH)

Activity 1:

Performing traditional songs from the Teochew songbook: Two Degrees of Plum;The White Haired Girl Local traditional Musical Instruments, large gongs and drums, and Su gongs and drums can be used to assist

Activity 2:

Interactive mini games: Send gifts; Experience playing an instrument
